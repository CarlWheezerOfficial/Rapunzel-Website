Rapunzel

Re-Written


This is an interactive story based off of the Grimms fairytale Rapunzel.


Instructions

You will be given an explanation on your surrounding and what's happening.

You will be provided with a few choices.

Your actions will be described.


Storyline

This is a simplified retelling of the classic Rapunzel story. There are some changes and missing characters, but the base plot is till there. Have fun!
